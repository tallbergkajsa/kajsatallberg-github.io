Design Challenges

Project Overview

Throughout the course User Experience Design and Evaluation at KTH, we undertook four practical design challenges with industrial partners, conducted in quick design sprints in small groups. For each challenge, a representative from a company came to present a design brief which we were to solve and present within a short time frame. This year's representatives were from Spotify, King Games, Electrolux and Blast Theory. The challenges ranged from designing new opportunities for music discovery on Spotify and incorporating game design into a banking app, to enhancing the process of talking about sexual consent on Tinder and re-designing an interactive art experience.
