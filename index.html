Who am I

🇸🇪 Background

Stockholm born and raised, I early developed a passion for technology, guiding me toward The Royal Institute of Technology. Specifically fascinated by the interplay between technology and human interaction, I pursued a program in Interactive Media Technology. Ever since, I've been hooked on HCI, UX and Interaction Design, participating in various design courses and creative side projects. Seeking for international experiences, I also left my home base for an exchange semester in Melbourne, Australia.
